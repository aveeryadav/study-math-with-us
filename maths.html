📚 Class 10 Formulas - Chapter Wise

Chapter 1: Real Numbers

  • Euclid’s Division Lemma: a = bq + r
  • HCF × LCM = Product of two numbers

Chapter 2: Polynomials

  • Zeros of a quadratic polynomial: ax² + bx + c = 0
  • Relationship: α + β = -b/a, αβ = c/a

Chapter 3: Pair of Linear Equations

  • ax + by = c, dx + ey = f
  • Solution by substitution, elimination, cross-multiplication

Chapter 6: Triangles

  • Pythagoras Theorem: a² + b² = c²
  • Area of Similar Triangles = Ratio of Squares of Sides

Chapter 7: Coordinate Geometry

  • Distance formula: √[(x₂−x₁)² + (y₂−y₁)²]
  • Midpoint formula: [(x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2]
  • Section formula: [(mx₂+nx₁)/(m+n), (my₂+ny₁)/(m+n)]

Chapter 8: Introduction to Trigonometry

  • sinθ = Perpendicular / Hypotenuse
  • cosθ = Base / Hypotenuse
  • tanθ = Perpendicular / Base

Chapter 13: Surface Areas and Volumes

  • Cylinder Volume = πr²h
  • Sphere Volume = 4/3πr³
  • Cone Volume = 1/3πr²h

Chapter 14: Statistics

  • Mean = Σfx / Σf
  • Median (class) = l + [(n/2 – F)/f] × h
  • Mode = l + [(f₁ – f₀) / (2f₁ – f₀ – f₂)] × h

Chapter 15: Probability

  • P(E) = Number of favorable outcomes / Total outcomes
